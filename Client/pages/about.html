Our Team Objectives




The objective of our models is to equip the farmers with tailored recommendations for crop varieties that suit their unique soil and climate conditions. By leveraging various data inputs such as soil nutrient levels, pH, crop nutrient requirements, and environmental factors, farmers can optimize their fertilizer usage. This not only minimizes nutrient runoff and leaching but also mitigates the risk of water pollution and eutrophication.
